**Music Collaboration Analysis Dashboard: Chris Brown ft. Davido | Power BI**

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze the musical collaborations between Chris Brown and Davido. The dashboard provides insights into song performance, streaming trends, audience engagement, and collaboration success across various digital music platforms.

**Key Features**:
-Analyzed the performance of collaborative tracks based on streams, views, and popularity metrics.
-Monitored trends in song releases, listener engagement, and streaming growth over time.
-Evaluated top-performing songs based on plays, likes, and audience interactions.
-Compared the performance of collaborations across different music platforms and regions.
-Tracked key KPIs such as total streams, average engagement, and top-charting tracks.
-Created interactive visualizations with filters, drill-through capabilities, and dynamic KPI cards for detailed analysis.
**Tools & Technologies:**
-Power BI
-Power Query (ETL)
-DAX
-Data Modeling
-Interactive Data Visualization
**Business Impact:**

Enabled music analysts and stakeholders to understand audience preferences, measure the success of artist collaborations, identify high-performing tracks, and support data-driven decisions for future music releases and marketing campaigns.
